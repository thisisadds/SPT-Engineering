Case Study 2

Database logic and process optimisation for vehicle manufacturing

The key to good data analysis is accurate and real time source data with minimal process time, so that a clear picture of events can be seen and acted upon.

A leading recreational vehicle manufacturer had assigned the project to conduct a detailed analysis of the types of warranties to compare with production quality. The aim was improve the accuracy of the data analysis, prove the link between other source factors that can affect production quality and highlight areas for improvement.

A detailed study was conducted of the human processes involved in input of the source data and its extraction from the database in the procurement, after sale warranties and production departments. This was followed by a data comparison from numerous Database generated reports and study of the source data reporting process.

Result: The project resulted in the database logic being redesigned to bring the reports into alignment and eliminate inaccuracies. The resulting data analysis standardisation between departments, improved processes and skill sharing, a new standardised electronic system resulted in a significant increase in communication between departments, leading to an increase in workflow efficiency and reduction in non-recurring costs.
