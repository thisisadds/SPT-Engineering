Computer Aided Design

Computer Aided Design is the most vital element of any product design. It significantly increases the productivity of engineering a product and enables engineers to visualise it digitally without the need for it to exist physically, so changes can be made as required. This also enables the users to easily design better with the end user in mind.

We realise this importance and our experience from past industries where CAD is essential to design helps us deliver an excellent service to our clients.

What we offer?

Our experience allows us to design and manage complex parts and assemblies with ease. We offer:

  • Parts and assembly design
  • 2D to 3D modelling and drafting
  • Digital mock-ups (DMUs)
  • Design integration activities
  • Motion analysis
  • Product configuration management
