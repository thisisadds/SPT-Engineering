Aerodynamics & Fluids

Optimising design for aerodynamic efficiency is often a key requirement of a product such as an aircraft, car or a wind turbine. However, aerodynamics and fluid mechanics are also essential for other various uses such as pipe flow, rocket nozzle design, intake design and much more.

What we offer?

Our experience and expertise allows us to help our clients with analysing and optimising aerodynamic performance of their component, product or assemblies. We offer:

  • Aerodynamic design and shape optimisation
  • Numerical and computation analysis
  • Aerodynamic loads generation and analysis
